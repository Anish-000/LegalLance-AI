**Legal Lance AI** is an AI-powered web application designed to simplify complex legal documents such as **Terms & Conditions**, **Privacy Policies**, and similar agreements. It helps users clearly understand what they are agreeing to by converting dense, messy legal text into a readable, structured, and insightful format.
